Overview

"Acme Clothing" is an in-development personal project I'm working on. It's meant to be an online e-commerce store for clothes and related products

It's still very early in development, and as of writing this, hasn't been worked on for more than a day, but I aim to make it as complete and versatile a Shopify theme as possible; incorporating as many of Shopify's core features as possible, and making it super customizable, which is admittedly not too easy of a task, since there aren't that many variations of a core design scheme.
